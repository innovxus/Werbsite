Our Past Events

08 th September 2023
InnovXus : Laid Its Foundation on September 8, 2023

Founded on September 8, 2023, by Sheetal Anand, Vikram Razz, and Vishnu Prakash , InnovXus began its journey with a mission to drive innovation and shape the future of technology. Built on shared vision and passion.

07 th October 2023
COD-A-FESTx 1.0

COD-A-FESTX 1.0 was a milestone hackathon, bringing together over 350 students passionate about tech and innovation. Designed to inspire and challenge, the event created a collaborative environment for participants to tackle real-world problems, share knowledge, and push the boundaries of creativity and coding.

14-22 thOctober 2023
MGL Box Cricket

The MGL Box Cricket League brought together over 25 teams and more than 250 players, creating an electrifying atmosphere filled with fierce competition and camaraderie. With each match, players showcased their skills, teamwork, and passion, making the league a thrilling celebration of cricket and sportsmanship.

16 th Feburary 2024
Cyber Drill

The Cyber Drill Workshop gathered over 150 participants, all eager to dive into the world of cybersecurity. Through discussions and hands-on activities, attendees explored essential strategies to protect against cyber threats, making it a powerful session for building knowledge and resilience in the digital landscape.

22 nd February 2024
HackEthix

HackEthix, an ethical hacking workshop, drew an impressive crowd of over 300 students eager to explore the realm of cybersecurity. The workshop provided hands-on experience and in-depth knowledge, empowering participants with ethical hacking skills to protect and secure digital environments.

18 thAugust 2024
Amritsar Trip : History and Heritage

Our Amritsar trip was a blend of history and spirituality. We visited Jallianwala Bagh, experienced the Wagah Border ceremony, found peace at the Golden Temple, enjoyed local delicacies at Amritsar Haveli, and explored Ram Tirth Sthal, connecting deeply with India's heritage.

30 th August 2024
Paris Olympic Celebration

The Parish Olympian Celebration featured a thrilling bike rally that brought together riders of all ages, united by a common passion for sports and fitness. The rally was an energetic display of enthusiasm, with participants cruising through the streets, promoting unity, health, and the Olympic spirit.

5 th September 2024
Tribute to Dr. Sarvepalli Radha Krishnan

On Teachers' Day, we organized a vibrant event at the School of Computer Applications to honor the educators who inspire and shape our futures. The celebration featured a variety of competitions, including dancing, singing, and poetry, where students showcased their talents in creative expressions.

7th September 2024
COD-A-FESTX 2.0

COD-A-FESTX 2.0 was a phenomenal success, featuring over 40 teams and more than 200 participants. The event brought together tech enthusiasts, innovators, and problem-solvers who collaborated and competed to showcase their skills in a dynamic hackathon environment.
